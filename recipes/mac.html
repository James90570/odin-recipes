Shells & Cheese with bacon!

Serving a cheesy dish with a little grease!

List of ingredients & utensils:

  • A box of shells & cheese from local groccey store.
  • One small pack of bacon.
  • Small pot
  • Small pan
  • Small bowl.
  • Wooden spoon or a utensile to stir the shells & cheese.
  • Spatula or tongs.
  • Knife or cutting utensile
  • Cutting board of some sort.
Steps:
  1. Grab your box of shells & cheese then pour the box into the pan (not including the pack of cheese the box comes with)
  2. Fill the pot with water until all pasta is covered; proceed to cooking instructions on packaging.
  3. While the pasta is cooking grab the pack of bacon & place onto cutting board
  4. with the knife dice the bacon.
  5. After cutting place the diced bacon onto small pan & cook to your liking.
  6. Wait paitently unitl the pasta is finished; once finished empty the pot of pasta into a strainer to remove excesse water
  7. Place pasta into the small bowl & empty package of cheese into the bowl as well
  8. Mix the cheese with the pasta until creamy.
  9. When the bacon is done discard of the left over grease; then place bacon into the same bowl and the cheese & pasta.
  10. Mix until satisified.
  11. Discard of left over trash & place utensile away.
I enjoy eating with bread or crackers!

Enjoy!
