Peanut butter sandwhich with a twist

Making a peanut butter sandwhich including nutella

List of ingredients & utensils:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Bread
  • nutella
  • fork or butter knife
Steps:
  1. Grab your choice of bread (two slices is recommended)
  2. Get the peanut butter and nutella.
  3. With the utensils dip it into the peanut butter & spread it along the one slice of bread.
  4. After done with previous step wipe the utensils off (or don't) & proceed to spread nutella along the empty slice of bread.
  5. Place the utensils in a location that it will get washed.
  6. Put all ingredients away.
  7. Finally put both slices of bread together with the spread facing each other & enojoy.
I prefer my sandwhich with a glass of chocolate milk.

Enjoy!
