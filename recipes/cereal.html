Cereal! (yup that's it)

A delicious bowl of cereal!

List of ingredients & utensils:

  • A choice of your boxed cereal (no judgement)
  • Milk of your choice (again no judgement)
  • Spoon
  • Bowl
Steps:
  1. Grab your bowl & cereal of choice; pour the cereal into the bowl as much as you'd like (save some room for the milk)
  2. After the milk into the bowl of cereal.
  3. Place spoon inside of bowl
  4. And that's it!
  5. Put milk and box of cereal away.
I also add bannas for a little sweetness!

Enjoy!
